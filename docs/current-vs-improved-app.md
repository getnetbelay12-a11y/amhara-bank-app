# Current vs Improved App

## Current Live App Strengths To Preserve

The current Amhara Bank mobile experience already appears strong in the daily-banking areas that customers expect first:

- balance and account visibility
- mini statement and transaction history
- transfers
- airtime top-up
- utility and school payments
- exchange rate
- loan calculator
- donation
- profile, security, and help access

These strengths should stay visible in the improved platform. The redesign goal is not to remove them. The goal is to place them more clearly, connect them to backend workflows, and reduce clutter.

## What Should Be Preserved

- a fast daily-banking home centered on balance and common actions
- clean access to payments and transfers
- transaction history and mini statement visibility
- self-service profile and security controls
- the Amhara Bank blue, yellow, and white brand language

## What Should Be Simplified

- Home should stop acting like a feature catalog
- old `My Bank` overflow should be redistributed into clearer sections
- support tools should stop being scattered across multiple surfaces
- payment features should be grouped by customer intent instead of by technical implementation
- shareholder features should be hidden for non-shareholders

## What Should Be Newly Added Or Strengthened

- working live chat across mobile, backend, and console
- a full notification center with push-first delivery and deep links
- loan workflow tracking with document-request handling
- full onboarding and KYC with Fayda plus selfie
- recurring payment and standing-instruction experiences
- smart reminders and personalized insights
- role-based console operations for branch, district, and head office

## What Should Be Role-Based Only

- shareholder dashboard
- shareholder announcements
- voting and governance actions
- head-office governance console
- district and branch scoped dashboards and queues

## Improved Platform Structure

### Mobile

- `Home`
  - greeting
  - balance card
  - 4 quick actions
  - smart reminders only when relevant
  - recent activity preview
- `Payments`
  - daily payments
  - transfers and requests
  - beneficiaries
  - school pay
  - recurring payments
- `Transactions`
  - mini statement
  - transaction history
  - search and filters
- `Support`
  - live chat
  - ABa Care Center
  - FAQ
  - branch and ATM locator
- `Profile`
  - profile and member details
  - security controls
  - services and utilities
  - legal and settings
  - shareholder services when eligible

### Console

- head office dashboards and governance
- district performance and escalations
- branch operations and support queues
- notification campaign console
- loan workflow console
- live support workspace

### Backend

- event-driven notifications
- service requests and dispute workflows
- loan workflow state machine
- shared uploads and document evidence handling
- audit and risk logging
- reusable feature-flag and role-gating patterns

## Outcome

The improved app should feel like a modern digital banking platform built on top of the current live app strengths, not a disconnected redesign. Daily banking stays familiar. Workflow, support, notifications, and operations become much stronger.
